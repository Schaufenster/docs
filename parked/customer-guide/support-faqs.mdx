Can't find what you need? This page covers the most common customer questions and explains how to get in touch with your dedicated support team.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if my order is late?">
    Your Key Account Manager will proactively notify you if there's a known delay with your delivery. If your order is late and you haven't heard anything, contact your KAM directly by phone or email. You can also submit a support ticket via the **Help** button inside the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my order after confirming it?">
    Yes — you can modify your order up to **24 hours before** the scheduled delivery time. To make changes, go to **Orders**, find the relevant order, and click **Edit**. After the 24-hour cut-off, the caterer will already be preparing your food and changes may no longer be possible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I request a refund or credit?">
    Contact your KAM directly or submit a support ticket via the **Help** button in the platform. Please include your order number and a description of the issue. Refunds are reviewed on a case-by-case basis and are typically processed within **5–7 business days** of approval.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What dietary options are available?">
    The dietary options available to you depend on the catering partners in your area. When browsing caterers, use the dietary filter to surface options that match your team's requirements — including vegan, vegetarian, halal, gluten-free, nut-free, and more. You can also save your preferred dietary flags in **Settings → Preferences** so they're applied automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I order from multiple caterers in one delivery?">
    Currently, each order is fulfilled by a single catering partner. If you'd like food from more than one caterer for the same date, you can place multiple separate orders. Your KAM can help you coordinate this for larger events.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my account?">
    To close your Egora account, contact your KAM to initiate an offboarding process. We'll make sure any outstanding or recurring orders are handled appropriately before your account is closed, and that your final invoice is accurate and complete.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting help

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contact Your KAM" icon="user-tie">
    Your Key Account Manager is your dedicated point of contact for everything account-related — special requests, billing questions, order issues, and more. Their contact details are available in the platform under **Settings → My Account**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Submit a Support Ticket" icon="ticket">
    For platform issues or non-urgent queries, use the **Help** button in the Egora platform. A member of the support team will respond as quickly as possible.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  For urgent issues — such as a problem with a delivery that's happening today — call your KAM directly. Don't wait for a ticket response when time is critical.
</Tip>
