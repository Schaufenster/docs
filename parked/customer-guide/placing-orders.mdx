Egora gives you full control over your catering orders — from one-off team lunches to weekly recurring meals. This guide explains everything you need to know about ordering.

## Placing a one-time order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Orders → New Order">
    From your dashboard, navigate to **Orders** in the main menu and select **New Order**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your caterer and date">
    Browse available caterers and choose the one you'd like to order from. Then pick your preferred delivery date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose menu items and quantities">
    Select the dishes you want and set the quantity for each item. Menu item descriptions include allergen and dietary information to help you choose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add special instructions or dietary notes">
    Use the notes field to flag specific requirements — for example, "please label vegan dishes separately" or "leave at reception, call on arrival."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    Check your order summary, delivery time, and total cost. Click **Confirm Order** to submit. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting up a recurring order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Follow the same steps as a one-time order">
    Build your order exactly as described above — select your caterer, date, items, and any special instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle 'Repeat this order'">
    On the order confirmation page, enable the **Repeat this order** toggle before submitting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your frequency">
    Choose how often you'd like the order to repeat: **weekly**, **bi-weekly**, or **monthly**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an end date or leave open-ended">
    Set an end date if your program has a fixed duration, or leave it open-ended. You can pause or cancel a recurring order at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your recurring order">
    Click **Save & Confirm** to activate the recurring schedule. Each upcoming instance will appear in your Orders list and you'll receive a reminder email before each delivery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Modifying or cancelling an order

You can edit or cancel any upcoming order directly from the **Orders** page. Locate the order, click the three-dot menu, and select **Edit** or **Cancel**.

**Cut-off times apply** — all changes must be made at least **24 hours before** the scheduled delivery time. After the cut-off, modifications may not be possible as the caterer will already be preparing your order.

<Warning>
  Cancellations made within 24 hours of delivery may incur a late cancellation fee. Review your agreement or contact your KAM if you're unsure about the policy for your account.
</Warning>

## Order tracking

Once your order is confirmed, you can track its progress in real time from the **Orders** page. Here's what each status label means:

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Confirmed** | Your order has been received and accepted by the caterer. |
| **In Preparation** | The caterer is actively preparing your food. |
| **Out for Delivery** | Your order is on its way to your delivery address. |
| **Delivered** | Your order has been marked as delivered. |

You'll receive an email notification at each stage, so you can stay informed without needing to log in.

## Giving feedback

After each delivery, you'll receive a feedback prompt by email and in the platform. Take a moment to rate your experience — your ratings help Egora match you with the best catering partners over time.

If you experienced an issue with your order, contact your KAM directly or submit a support ticket via the **Help** button in the platform. Don't wait for the feedback prompt if something needs urgent attention.

<Tip>
  For large events (50+ people), contact your KAM at least 5 business days in advance to ensure availability and give caterers enough time to prepare.
</Tip>
