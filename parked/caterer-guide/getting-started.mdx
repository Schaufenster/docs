Getting set up on Egora is straightforward. Once your KAM has invited you, you'll complete your profile, upload your menu, and be ready to receive orders.

## Setting up your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept your invitation">
    You'll receive an email from your Egora KAM with a personalised activation link. Click the link to activate your caterer account and set your password. The link expires after 48 hours — if it has lapsed, ask your KAM to resend it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add your business name, logo, contact details, and a short description of your offering. This information appears on your public caterer listing, so make it clear, professional, and accurate. Clients often make decisions based on your profile before they ever see your menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your service area and delivery windows">
    Define the postcodes or delivery zones you can reliably service, and configure your available delivery time slots (e.g., 11:30–12:00, 12:00–12:30). Only commit to windows and areas you can consistently cover — overpromising here leads to fulfilment issues down the line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your menu">
    Add menu items with names, descriptions, photos, prices, and dietary tags. Take your time here — accurate, well-presented menus reduce customer complaints and increase the likelihood of selection. Every item should have enough detail for a client to make a confident choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set minimum order values">
    Define the minimum spend required per order to ensure every delivery is commercially viable for your business. Your KAM can advise on what's typical for your market and order volume.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a test order">
    Before you go live, your KAM will place a test order to verify that your menu, availability, and confirmation workflow are all functioning correctly. This is your chance to catch any gaps before real clients start ordering.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding your dashboard

Once you're live, your dashboard is your central hub for everything on Egora. Here's what each section does:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Orders" icon="list-check">
    View incoming, confirmed, and completed orders. This is where you confirm new orders and update their status throughout fulfilment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Menu" icon="utensils">
    Add, edit, and manage your menu items, categories, dietary tags, and availability schedules.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments" icon="sterling-sign">
    Track your monthly payment summaries, view fulfilled order totals, and download invoices.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Messages" icon="envelope">
    Communicate directly with your KAM and receive platform notifications about your account.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Menu items without photos get fewer selections. We recommend uploading high-quality images for every item.
</Note>
