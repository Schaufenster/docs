Claude Teams is Anthropic's AI assistant for enterprise teams. When connected to the Egora KAM Knowledge Base, Claude can answer your account management questions with accurate, up-to-date context — from escalation procedures to customer order history.

## Why use Claude Teams with the KB?

Connecting Claude Teams to the KB gives your team a shortcut to the right answer at the right moment. Instead of searching through Notion pages or Slack threads, you ask Claude directly.

## Setting up the connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your KB API key">
    Go to **Settings → API Access** in the KB admin panel and generate a new API key. Copy it and store it securely — it won't be shown again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Claude Teams settings">
    In your Claude Teams workspace, navigate to **Knowledge Sources → Add Source**. You need workspace admin permissions to complete this step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the KB endpoint">
    Paste the KB's public API URL and your API key into the fields provided. Use the following format:

    ```text
    URL: https://kb.egora.com/api/v1/knowledge
    API Key: your-kb-api-key
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select content scope">
    Choose which KB sections Claude can access. Available sections include **KAM Processes**, **Customer Guide**, and **Gleap Digest**. Restrict access to the sections relevant to your team's role — this keeps responses focused and reduces noise.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the connection">
    Ask Claude the following question to confirm the KB is live:

    > "What is the escalation process for a delivery failure?"

    If Claude returns a correct, detailed answer sourced from the KB, the connection is working. If it responds with a generic answer, re-check your API key and endpoint URL.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example prompts for KAMs

Once Claude is connected to the KB, use these prompts to get the most out of it during your daily work:

- "Summarise the recent Gleap tickets for [Account Name]"
- "What is the offboarding checklist for a customer?"
- "What changed in the platform this week that affects customers?"
- "Draft a follow-up email to a customer after a late delivery"
- "What's the SLA for responding to a food quality complaint?"
- "Find all escalation tickets from last month"
- "Give me a pre-call briefing for my meeting with [Account Name] tomorrow"
- "What is the process for handling a caterer who misses an order deadline?"

## Privacy and access

Claude queries are logged in your Claude Teams workspace. Keep the following in mind when using Claude with KB data:

- **Do not paste personally identifiable information (PII)** into Claude prompts beyond what the KB already provides. The KB surfaces anonymised or role-appropriate data — use that.
- If you need account-specific detail, reference the account by name rather than pasting raw data from other systems.
- Your workspace admin can audit query logs at any time. Treat Claude interactions as you would any internal business tool.

<Tip>
  Create a shared Claude Teams prompt library with your team's most-used KB queries. This saves time and ensures consistency across KAMs when pulling account briefings, escalation paths, or process steps.
</Tip>
