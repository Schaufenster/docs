Notion remains where KAMs keep **working, in-flux** documentation. We do **not** sync Notion into this KB
automatically. Instead:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Claude reads Notion live" icon="robot">
    Via the claude.ai **Notion connector**, the assistant can read the latest Notion drafts directly —
    no sync, always current.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stable docs get promoted" icon="arrow-up">
    When a Notion doc hardens into "official" process, it's moved into this KB (git-versioned, reviewed).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why this split

- **Notion** = fast, messy, latest thinking → read live for "what's the newest draft?".
- **This KB** = curated, reviewed, official → the source of truth for "how do we actually do X?".

<Note>
  Mintlify also offers a native Notion connector for its Agent. We are evaluating it during the test, but
  the default model for now is **N1: Notion stays live, Claude reads it directly.**
</Note>
