Egora is a food-tech platform that connects corporate clients with professional caterers, streamlining everything from order placement to fulfilment. The **KAM Knowledge Base** is the central hub where Key Account Managers find every process guide, playbook, customer resource, and caterer reference they need — all in one place, always up to date.

## What is the KAM Knowledge Base?

The KAM Knowledge Base consolidates information from across Egora's operational stack into a single, searchable source of truth. Instead of hunting through scattered Notion pages, digging through Slack history, or chasing down Git commit messages, KAMs have everything surfaced and structured here automatically.

Content is aggregated from four primary sources:

- **Notion** — process documentation, SOPs, and team wikis
- **Slack** — relevant threads, decisions, and announcements
- **Git** — commit messages and change logs that affect workflows
- **Gleap** — customer messages, feedback, and support tickets

The result is that KAMs always have up-to-date context — whether they're onboarding a new account, troubleshooting a customer issue, or briefing a caterer on platform changes.

## How it Powers Your Work

The KB isn't just a static document library — it's a living system designed to make every part of your KAM workflow faster and more informed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sources Sync Automatically">
    Notion pages, Slack threads, Git commits, and Gleap messages are continuously ingested and indexed. There's no manual copy-paste; content arrives here as it's created or updated at the source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Content is Searchable and Structured">
    Every piece of content is organised into sections — KAM Handbook, Customer Guide, Caterer Guide, and Integrations — and is fully searchable so you can find exactly what you need in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claude Teams Gets Full Context">
    Because the KB is the underlying data source for Claude Teams, the AI assistant can answer account-specific questions, summarise customer history, and suggest next steps with full awareness of your latest processes and communications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Help Desk Articles Are Kept Fresh">
    Customer-facing and caterer-facing help articles are generated and refreshed directly from KB content, so your help desk always reflects the current state of the platform without manual updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Bookmark the **Escalation Playbook** in the KAM Handbook for quick access during live customer calls. It covers decision trees, SLA commitments, and escalation contacts so you can respond confidently under pressure.
</Tip>
