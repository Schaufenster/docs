## How the Egora platform works for caterers

Egora connects corporate clients with vetted catering partners — and your role is at the heart of that experience. When a client places an order, it lands directly in your Egora dashboard. You confirm the order, prepare the food, and handle delivery to the client's location. Payment is processed automatically on a monthly cycle, so you never need to chase invoices or manage billing manually.

The platform is designed to keep things simple: one place for orders, menus, payments, and communication.

## What makes a great Egora catering partner

Corporate clients have high expectations around consistency, dietary compliance, and punctuality. The partners who perform best on Egora share a few key qualities:

- **Menu accuracy** — Your menu reflects exactly what you can deliver, with up-to-date items, prices, and dietary information
- **On-time delivery** — You arrive within the agreed delivery window, every time
- **Responsive communication** — You confirm orders promptly and flag any issues early
- **Dietary compliance** — You take allergen and dietary requirements seriously and fulfil them without error
- **Presentation and quality** — Food arrives well-packaged, labelled, and at the right temperature

Meeting these standards consistently builds your rating on the platform and leads to more order volume over time.
