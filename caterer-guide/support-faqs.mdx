Have a question about an order, payment, or your account? This page answers the most common queries from Egora catering partners and explains how to get fast support.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When and how do I get paid?">
    Payments are processed monthly for all fulfilled orders from the previous calendar month. You'll receive an automated payment summary by email, followed by a bank transfer to your registered account by the **10th of each month**.

    You can view a breakdown of fulfilled orders and expected earnings at any time in the **Payments** tab of your dashboard. If a payment hasn't arrived by the 11th, contact your KAM with your payment reference.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I need to cancel an order?">
    Contact your KAM immediately — before taking any action in the platform. Your KAM will assess the situation, notify the client, and advise on next steps. Do not cancel an order directly in the platform without prior authorisation.

    Cancelling without going through your KAM is a breach of your partner agreement and can result in account suspension. For more detail, see [Handling issues during fulfilment](/caterer-guide/fulfilling-orders#handling-issues-during-fulfilment).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A customer complained about their order — what do I do?">
    Your KAM will contact you with the details of the complaint. When they do:

    - Respond promptly — delays make complaints harder to resolve
    - Provide any relevant context: photos of the packed order, delivery records, or notes about the preparation
    - Be factual and professional, not defensive

    Complaints factor into your partner rating, so it's worth treating each one as an opportunity to improve. Do not contact the client directly about a complaint unless your KAM specifically advises it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my delivery windows or service area?">
    Yes. Go to your **Profile** settings and update your delivery windows or postcode/zone coverage. Changes take effect for new orders only — any orders that have already been confirmed under your existing settings are unaffected.

    If you're making a significant reduction to your service area or availability, give your KAM a heads-up so they can manage expectations with clients who order from you regularly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add a new cuisine type to my listing?">
    Update your menu by adding items in the new cuisine category (see [Adding menu items](/caterer-guide/managing-menu#adding-menu-items)). Once the items are live, contact your KAM to request a refresh of your listing tags so the new cuisine type appears correctly in client search filters.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is my partner rating based on?">
    Your partner rating is calculated from three key factors:

    - **On-time delivery rate** — The percentage of your orders delivered within the confirmed delivery window
    - **Customer feedback scores** — Ratings left by clients after each order is marked as delivered
    - **Order confirmation speed** — How quickly you confirm orders relative to the 2-hour window

    Your KAM can share your current rating breakdown at any time. A strong rating leads to more order volume and better placement in client search results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting support

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contact Your KAM" icon="user-tie">
    Your Key Account Manager is your primary point of contact for anything account-related — orders, payments, menu changes, complaints, or growth opportunities. Reach out via Slack or email. Your KAM's contact details are in the **Messages** tab of your dashboard.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Submit a Ticket" icon="ticket">
    For platform technical issues — bugs, login problems, or dashboard errors — use the **Gleap** widget in the bottom corner of your dashboard to submit a support ticket. The Egora platform team will respond within one business day.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  For urgent day-of issues — such as a van breakdown, sudden ingredient shortage, or anything that puts an active order at risk — call your KAM directly. Do not rely on email or tickets for urgent matters.
</Note>
