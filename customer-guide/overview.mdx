## How Egora works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse caterers and menus">
    Explore catering partners available in your area. Filter by cuisine type, dietary requirements, minimum order size, or rating to find the best fit for your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place and schedule orders">
    Select your caterer, choose menu items, set a delivery date and time, and confirm. You can place a one-off order or schedule a recurring meal program.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track delivery and give feedback">
    Follow your order from confirmation through to delivery. Once your meal arrives, share your feedback to help Egora match you with the best caterers going forward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can order

Egora supports a range of catering formats to suit your team's needs:

- **Daily office lunches** — Regular weekday meals delivered to your workplace.
- **Event catering** — Catering for company events, all-hands meetings, client lunches, and celebrations.
- **Recurring meal plans** — Weekly or bi-weekly programs with flexible scheduling and easy modification.
- **Dietary-specific menus** — Vegan, vegetarian, halal, gluten-free, nut-free, and more — filter by your team's requirements when browsing.

<Tip>
  Your Key Account Manager is your dedicated point of contact at Egora. Reach out to them directly for any special requests or account questions.
</Tip>
