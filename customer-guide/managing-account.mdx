Your Egora account gives you full control over your team's catering setup — from inviting colleagues to reviewing monthly spend and updating delivery addresses. All account settings are accessible from the **Settings** menu in the top-right corner of the platform.

## Managing team members

Admins can invite colleagues, assign roles, and remove access at any time from **Settings → Team**.

**To invite a new user:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Team">
    Navigate to **Settings** and select the **Team** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Invite Member'">
    Enter the person's email address and select their role — **Admin** or **Member**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send Invite**. The new user will receive an email with a link to activate their account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm their access">
    Once they've activated their account, they'll appear in your team list. You can change their role or remove them at any time from the same page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To remove a user, click the three-dot menu next to their name and select **Remove**. Their access will be revoked immediately.

## Billing and invoices

Egora invoices your account on a **monthly cycle** by default. Invoices consolidate all orders placed during the billing period and are sent to your account's billing email address on the first business day of the following month.

You can find all past invoices under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**. To update your payment details or switch to a purchase order (PO) process, go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**.

<Info>
  Invoice emails are sent to the billing address on file. If your finance team needs to receive invoices directly, update the billing email address under **Settings → Billing → Billing Contact** or ask your KAM to update it on your behalf.
</Info>

## Delivery addresses

Add as many delivery locations as your organisation needs under **Settings → Addresses**. This is useful if your team operates across multiple offices or floors.

For each address, you can configure:

- **Default contact name and phone number** — who the delivery driver should call on arrival.
- **Delivery instructions** — access codes, floor numbers, preferred drop-off points, or any other notes for the driver.

To add a new address, click **Add Address**, fill in the details, and save. You can set one address as your default, which will be pre-selected every time you place a new order.

## Dietary and preference settings

Set team-wide dietary flags under **Settings → Preferences**. Any requirements you add here — such as vegan, halal, nut-free, or gluten-free — will be automatically applied as filters whenever you browse caterers, ensuring you only see menus your team can eat.

You can still adjust these on a per-order basis if needed. Team-wide preferences act as a default, not a hard restriction.

## Viewing order history

Your full order history is available under **Orders → History**. You can filter by date range, caterer, or order status.

To export your order history for expense reporting or internal records, click **Export** and select **CSV**. The export includes order dates, caterer names, item breakdowns, and totals.

Past invoices are also available under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History** as downloadable PDFs.

<Note>
  Only Admins can change billing details or invite new users. If you need these changes and don't have Admin access, ask your KAM to make the update or to grant you Admin permissions.
</Note>
