eGora helps you find and book catering in **Berlin** and **München**. Here's the whole journey, so you
know what happens after you hit submit.

<Note>
  Sample customer-facing content for the pilot. Wording and details are illustrative.
</Note>

## At a glance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tell us about your event">
    Fill in the [catering request form](https://egora-catering.de/iframe/catering-form): occasion, date,
    number of guests, budget and any preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We match caterers">
    eGora matches your request to suitable caterers from our vetted network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You receive an offer">
    You get one or more offers with menus and pricing. A Key Account Manager is available if you have
    questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You book">
    Confirm the offer that fits. Payment and invoicing run through eGora.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delivery & enjoy">
    The caterer delivers on your service date. Afterwards, you can leave a review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you'll need ready

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Event basics" icon="calendar">
    Date, time, location (city), and approximate guest count.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Budget" icon="euro-sign">
    A per-person budget range helps us match the right caterers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Occasion & style" icon="utensils">
    E.g. business lunch, wedding, office catering — and any dietary needs (vegan, allergies).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact details" icon="address-card">
    So we (and the caterer) can reach you about your booking.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Frequently asked

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which cities does eGora serve?">
    Currently **Berlin** and **München**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a cancellation guarantee?">
    Many orders include a cancellation guarantee (Stornogarantie) whose terms scale with the order — the
    details appear on your offer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get help choosing?">
    Yes — a Key Account Manager can advise on caterers and menus before you book.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
