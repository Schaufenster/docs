## Getting Started as a New KAM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the Handbook">
    Start with the [KAM Handbook](/introduction) to understand how this knowledge base is structured, what each section covers, and where to find the processes that govern your day-to-day work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up the MCP Connector">
    Connect Claude Desktop to eGora's live data via the [MCP setup guide](/it/mcp-setup) so you can ask real questions about orders, requests, and accounts directly in chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Tools">
    Link your [Notion workspace](/integrations/notion-sync), [Slack channels](/integrations/slack-sync), and [Gleap account](/integrations/gleap-messages) so new content and customer messages are automatically surfaced here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using AI">
    Open [Claude Teams](/integrations/claude-teams-setup) and start asking questions. With your tools connected, Claude has full context from all your sources and can help you draft responses, summarise accounts, and look up processes instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This knowledge base is updated automatically from all connected sources — Notion docs, Slack threads, Git commits, and Gleap customer messages. You don't need to maintain it manually; just keep your integrations connected and the content stays fresh.
</Note>
