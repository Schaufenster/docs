This knowledge base is the central hub for everything KAMs at Egora need — from onboarding scripts to escalation playbooks, customer FAQs to caterer guides. Whether you're handling your first account or your fiftieth, everything you need to do your job confidently lives here.

## Why this KB exists

Before the KB, information was scattered across Notion pages, Slack DMs, and individual spreadsheets. Finding the right escalation path meant pinging three colleagues. Checking a caterer's SLA meant digging through a shared drive. Onboarding a new customer meant relying on whoever sat next to you.

<Info>
  The Egora KAM Knowledge Base was created to end that fragmentation. It consolidates all process documentation, customer guides, caterer resources, and integration references into one searchable, always-current source of truth — so you spend less time hunting for information and more time building relationships.
</Info>

## How content stays current

You don't need to maintain this KB manually. Content flows in automatically from every source your team already uses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Notion sync">
    Process documentation and internal guides are authored in Notion. Any time a Notion page is updated, the corresponding KB section syncs automatically — so you're always reading the latest version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Slack highlights">
    Important updates, policy changes, and quick fixes posted in designated Slack channels are captured and surfaced in the relevant KB sections. Nothing important gets buried in a thread.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Git commit tracking">
    When engineers ship product changes, commit messages and release notes are pulled into the KB — so you always know what changed in the platform before your customers ask about it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gleap customer messages">
    Real feedback and recurring questions from customers submitted via Gleap are reviewed and folded into FAQs and troubleshooting guides, adding authentic customer voice to the documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Search and AI

You can find any topic in this KB using the **search bar** at the top of the page. Search by process name, company name, issue type, or any keyword — results surface content from across all sections instantly.

Once Claude Teams is configured for your workspace, you can go further: ask questions in natural language directly in your Claude Teams interface. Instead of browsing, just ask — *"What's the SLA for a same-day catering cancellation?"* or *"Walk me through the payment dispute escalation path"* — and get an answer drawn directly from this KB.

<Tip>
  If something is missing or outdated, flag it in the **#knowledge-base** Slack channel. The KB owner reviews submissions weekly and will update the source doc or prioritise a new article.
</Tip>
