The analytics area. All four KPI menu entries link to the **same** Catering KPI page
(`/catering-kpi-page`), just anchored to different sections.

## KPI page

| Menu entry | Jump anchor | Content |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Request KPIs** | `#conversion-rate-kpis` | Conversion funnel (converted, completed, lost …) |
| **Marketing KPIs** | `#google-ads-kpis` | Google Ads funnel & cohorts |
| **Finance KPIs** | `#finance-kpis` | Revenue, commission, payouts, taxes |
| **Diverse KPIs** | `#backend-visit-rate-kpis` | Backend usage, caterer / customer interaction |

<Note>
  The KPI page defines **~109 canonical metric tiles** across 12 sections. It's the source of truth for
  "how high is our X?". The full list is in the [KPI catalog](/en/it/kpi-catalog); the Claude assistant
  reads the same values server-side via the `get_catering_kpi` MCP function.
</Note>
