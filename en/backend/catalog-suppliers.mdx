This is where you manage the **caterer side** of the marketplace – who delivers, from which location, on
what terms, and what is in the catalog.

## Caterers & branches

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Retailer" icon="building-user">
    A **caterer** (marketplace partner). Has branches, products, packages and contact people; carries
    scores and capability taxonomies used by matching.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Branch" icon="location-dot">
    A **caterer location** (kitchen). A retailer can have multiple branches – each with opening hours,
    minimum order value, take rate and delivery area. Orders and offers attach to a branch.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Billing & payout

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="MonthlyRetailerBill" icon="file-invoice-dollar">
    The monthly **credit note** per branch/month (with PDF). Aggregates payouts, fees and manual
    adjustments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="PayoutAdjustment" icon="sliders">
    Manual correction items on a monthly statement. Gross and tax are calculated automatically from the
    net amount × tax rate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The monthly credit note has several payout components (including deposit and the egora voucher share).
  If individual items and the total don't reconcile, there is a reconcile command
  (`audit:monthly-payout-reconcile`).
</Note>

## Catalog

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Product" icon="box">
    A catalog product of a caterer. Carries variants, options, categories and allergens.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Productvariant" icon="boxes-stacked">
    The actually sellable unit of a product – this is what ends up in offers and orders.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Package" icon="box-open">
    A bundled, priced catering package of a caterer (menu including surcharges for drinks, equipment,
    service, number of people).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Voucher" icon="ticket">
    Vouchers; the reimbursement can be split between egora and the caterer.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Product categories drive the **tax rate** (via the category default rate). When you create or change a
  category inline, make sure to pick the correct leaf category rather than the menu group.
</Tip>
