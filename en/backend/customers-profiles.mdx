Three related KAM areas: **Users & Customers**, **Smart Profiles** and **Feedback & Surveys**.

## Users & Customers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User" icon="user">
    A platform user account (backend/platform access).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Company" icon="building">
    A **B2B account** that bundles users/customers. Has orders, requests, billing profiles and email
    domains; carries an account owner (KAM).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The **Customer** (the individual orderer) isn't surfaced as its own menu item for KAMs – you reach
  customer data via the request/order. A request carries an `is_b2b` flag; beyond that flag, the
  distinction between customer and company isn't spelled out in code.
</Note>

## Smart Profiles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="BillingProfile" icon="address-card">
    Billing profile (invoice address/details) attached to a customer or a company.
  </Card>
  <Card title="EventFormat" icon="calendar-star">
    Recurring event formats of a customer/company (e.g. typical occasions, budget range as min/max).
    Maintained both in the KAM backend and in the customer-facing Filament backend.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Feedback & Surveys

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="SurveyInstance" icon="clipboard-question">
    A specific survey that has been sent out (to a customer, caterer or KAM) – including multi-order-hub
    surveys.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SurveyAnswer" icon="comment-dots">
    The individual answers to a survey instance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Survey-Insights" icon="chart-simple">
    The dashboard with aggregated ratings – can also be broken out per caterer.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Lost/completed requests automatically trigger a survey (see
  [When it goes sideways](/kam-handbook/when-it-goes-sideways)).
</Tip>
