Three related KAM sections: **Users & Customers**, **Smart Profiles** and **Feedback & Surveys**.

## Users & Customers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User" icon="user">
    A platform user account (backend / platform access).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Company" icon="building">
    A **B2B account** that bundles users / customers. Has orders, requests, billing profiles and
    email domains; carries an account owner (KAM).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The **Customer** (the individual orderer) is not exposed to KAMs as its own menu item – you reach
  customer data via the request or order. A request carries an `is_b2b` flag; beyond that, the
  Customer ↔ Company distinction is not spelled out in code.
</Note>

## Smart Profiles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="BillingProfile" icon="address-card">
    A billing profile (invoice address / data) attached to a customer or a company.
  </Card>
  <Card title="EventFormat" icon="calendar-star">
    Recurring event formats of a customer / company (e.g. typical occasions, budget range as min/max).
    Maintained both in the KAM backend and in the customer Filament backend.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Feedback & Surveys

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="SurveyInstance" icon="clipboard-question">
    A concretely sent survey (to a customer, caterer or KAM) – including multi-order hub surveys.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SurveyAnswer" icon="comment-dots">
    The individual answers belonging to a survey instance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Survey insights" icon="chart-simple">
    The dashboard with aggregated ratings – also breakable down per caterer.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Lost / completed requests automatically trigger a survey (see
  [When it goes sideways](/en/kam-handbook/when-it-goes-sideways)).
</Tip>
