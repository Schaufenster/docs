Notion remains the place where KAMs maintain their **in-progress, changing** documentation. We do **not**
auto-sync Notion into this KB. Instead:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Claude reads Notion live" icon="robot">
    Through claude.ai's **Notion connector**, the assistant can read the latest Notion drafts directly —
    no sync, always current.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stable documents are promoted" icon="arrow-up">
    Once a Notion document becomes an "official" process, it moves into this KB (git-versioned, reviewed).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why this split

- **Notion** = fast, messy, latest state → read live for "what is the newest draft?".
- **This KB** = curated, reviewed, official → the single source of truth for "how do we actually do X?".

<Note>
  Mintlify also offers a native Notion connector for its agent. We are evaluating it during the trial, but
  the default model for now is **N1: Notion stays live, Claude reads it directly.**
</Note>
