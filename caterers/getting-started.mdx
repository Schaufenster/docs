eGora connects caterers in **Berlin** and **München** with customers looking for catering. This page
covers the basics of getting set up.

<Note>
  Sample caterer-facing content for the pilot. Wording and details are illustrative.
</Note>

## How it works for caterers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get onboarded">
    Your eGora contact sets up your caterer profile and branch (location) details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your menu">
    Add your products and packages with categories and prices. Good categorisation drives correct VAT
    and better matching.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive matched requests">
    When a customer request fits your profile, eGora matches it to you — you don't chase leads.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send offers & deliver">
    Provide an offer; once booked, you fulfil the order on the service date. A delivery sheet
    (Lieferschein) accompanies each order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get paid">
    Payouts are handled through eGora on a regular billing cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting up your menu

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Products" icon="bowl-food">
    Individual dishes with a price and a leaf category (which determines VAT).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Packages" icon="box">
    Bundles of products offered as a set — useful for per-person catering.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Categories" icon="tags">
    Pick the most specific (leaf) category. A menu group is for display; the category drives tax.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Allergens" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Mark allergens on products so customers with dietary needs can be matched correctly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Category choice affects the tax rate applied to a line item. When in doubt about VAT, check with your
  eGora contact before publishing.
</Warning>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to find my own customers?">
    No — eGora matches suitable requests to your profile. You focus on great food and reliable delivery.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What appears on the delivery sheet?">
    The items for the order. Service-only lines such as the cancellation guarantee are not shown on the
    Lieferschein.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do payouts work?">
    eGora bills customers and pays caterers on a regular cycle — your contact can walk you through the
    specifics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
